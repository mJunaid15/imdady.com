Our Story

Imdady is the product brand of Futuregates which was founded in the year 2000 with the aim of providing IT services along with the cutting-edge Project Management services. In past two-decades, Futuregates has been successful in meeting the demands of its clients by providing them with a real-world solution. Our strategic IT consulting services will assist firms in automating and digitizing operations, optimizing software portfolios, and implementing cutting-edge technologies. We develop a complete IT strategy for your company's digital and technical transformation that is in line with its goals. Our work philosophy is straightforward- deliver practical, high-quality solutions. We provide vertical integration from requirement gathering through MVP development, testing, and deployment. We also assist companies in doing projects with strategic alignment that make the best use of the resources in the best budget.
